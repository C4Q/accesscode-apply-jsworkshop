Team Challenge

This exercise is meant to challenge you to not only put together everything you have learned to solve a new complex problem, but also to go beyond what we have taught you. In Access Code we will only be able to provide you the introduction and you will need to continue to deepen and broaden your understanding from there.

This exercise is also meant to challenge your ability to work as a team. We are less interested in seeing a perfect submission, and more interested in your group successfully working together.

What we want to see:

  • You have utilized the resources given to you during this workshop
  • You have researched and attempted to learn additional concepts
  • You have given careful thought to how to best solve the problem
  • Clear communication among team members
  • All team members contribute to a common solution
  • The team maintains a positive culture

The Task

Your team will be responsible for creating a Command Line Interface version of the game rock paper scissors.

A user should be able to play against the computer. Each “game” should be scored best 2 out of 3.

Instead of repl.it you will need to use jdoodle. Jdoodle will allow you to add interactivity to your game.

You will need to use a JS module called readline to get user input. Readline documentation.

Here is an example of a CLI game: Tic Tac Toe. Everything you see is taking place in the console. In this example the input “ruby bin/tictactoe” is the equivalent of hitting “run”.
